An organizational chart can be a difficult thing to understand, particularly for a large organization.

If we try to visualize the entire thing at once, for instance, the object is simply too complex to comprehend in any meaningful way.

Not to mention that the visualization itself is too resource-intensive to display with any real speed (taking several minutes to render and still not showing in its entirety).

Instead we'll try a guided search approach.

Enter one or two search terms and follow the dots.

Then scroll to see the teams that those people are a part of to put everything in context.
