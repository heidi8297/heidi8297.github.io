About Me

I am passionate about telling data-driven stories that inform decision-making and inspire meaningful action. I light up at the intersection of challenging, meaningful problems and creative, original solutions.

I'm very relationship-driven—I'm happier and do better work when I've taken the time to cultivate meaningful relationships with the people around me. If we're working together, I want to understand what you care about.

I love the unexpected and always strive to create moments of surprise and delight for my audience.

Things that matter to me: honesty, empathy, respect, collaboration, and human connection.
